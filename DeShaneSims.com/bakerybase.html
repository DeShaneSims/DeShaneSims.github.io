FAQ

1. How much time before my event should I place an order?
 
You should place an order at least a month in advance for big events,
but walk-ins are welcome for orders smaller than 150 people.

2. How long does it take to fulfill an order?
 
That depends on the amount of food and the intricacy of design. However, if you have an immediate need we do have premade delights, or we can fill the order with any treats of your choice. If still not satisfied we can offer a refund.

3. Can you do a dessert table?
 
We can fill a dessert table in our sleep. Don't worry if you don't know which desserts you want, we can fill it with samples or fill the rest of the table with repeat renditions of designated treats.

4. Do you have sample appointmens?
 
Yes, we can whip up a small portion of all flavors of cake, scone, latte, muffins etc.

5. What if I don't see what I want on the menu?
 
Please call us. We are always keeping up with baking trends, the menu is based on what we serve on the daily.
